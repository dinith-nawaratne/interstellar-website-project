Why Interstellar?

Interstellar is my favourite movie! Combining a very emotional story line with themes of love and sacrifice alongside complex and scientific concepts such as relativity, the nature of time, and the survival of the human race, Interstellar is bound to keep you on the edge of your seat throughout the entire movie. Not to mention the stunning graphics which can be seen throughout the film, from the cornfield in the opening scenes, to Miller's Planet and the scientifically accurate render of a blackhole, Gargantua. Additionally, the film score (composed by Hans Zimmer) has a very spacious and atmospheric sound to it, and is my favourite movie soundtrack of all time! Each piece, each instrument, and each note combine together so fluidly to create a masterpiece. Overall, Interstellar is one movie that resonates with not only me, but many audiences long after the credits roll!
